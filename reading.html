生词


课文

  地球从诞生到现在约有46亿年历史了，在漫长的地质历史时期，孕育了生命，形成了山川秀美的世界。那些奇山秀水及保存在岩石中的生命遗迹和地质构造遗迹都是地球历史的天然记录，统称为地质地貌景观。

 The earth is about 4.6 billion years old since its birth, and in the long period of geological history, life has been nurtured and a world of beautiful mountains and rivers has been formed. The relics of life and geological formations preserved in the rocks are all natural records of the earth's history, collectively known as geological landscapes. 

  风是塑造地表景观的动力之一。风沙地貌主要分布在地球南、北回归线两边的热带和亚热带地区以及温常大陆内部。其形成原因，是受到热带高压的控制，雨水很少，气候干燥，比如撒哈拉大沙漠(北非);或者是在大陆内部，离海遥远，潮湿的海洋气团无法到达，气候干旱，比如塔克拉玛干沙漠(中国)。 

  Wind is one of the driving forces that shape the landscape of the earth's surface. Aeolian sand landforms are mainly distributed in the tropical and subtropical regions on both sides of the Tropic of Cancer and the interior of the temperate continents. It is formed because it is controlled by tropical high pressures, with little rain and a dry climate, such as the Sahara Desert (North Africa); Or in the interior of the continent, far from the sea, inaccessible by humid ocean air masses, and in an arid climate, such as the Taklamakan Desert (China).  

  沙漠，是大自然留给人类的不幸之地。全世界有十分之一的陆地是沙漠。中国的沙漠面积约占全国面积的11%。世界上的沙漠大多分布在南北纬度15°~35°。这些地方气压高，风总是从陆地吹向海洋，海上的潮湿空气却进不到陆地上，因此雨量很少，非常干旱。地面上的岩石经过风化后形成细小的沙粒，沙粒堆积起来，就形成了沙丘，沙丘多了，慢慢就变成了沙漠。 

  The desert is an unfortunate place left by nature to mankind. One-tenth of the world's land mass is desert. China's desert area accounts for about 11% of the country's total area. Most of the deserts in the world are distributed in the north and south latitudes of 15°~35°. In these places, the air pressure is high, and the wind always blows from the land to the sea, but the humid air from the sea does not enter the land, so there is very little rainfall and it is very dry. The rocks on the ground are weathered to form fine grains of sand, and when the sand grains are piled up, sand dunes are formed, and when there are many dunes, they slowly become deserts. 

  沙丘是由风带着的沙粒在风变小或停下来时堆积而形成，形态有各种各样。有的如同弯弯的新月，有的像巨大的埃及金字塔，有的单个出现有的很多个连在一起。有些沙丘，当人在上面走或者向下滑动时，会发出奇怪的响声，许多沙漠中都有这种“沙鸣”现象。 

  Sand dunes are formed by the accumulation of sand particles carried by the wind when the wind becomes smaller or stops, and there are various forms. Some resemble a curved crescent, some resemble huge Egypt pyramids, and some are connected together in a single outfit. Some sand dunes make strange noises when people walk on them or slide down, and this "sand sound" phenomenon is found in many deserts. 

                                                              --改选自李烈荣、姜建军、王文主编的《中国地质遗迹资源及其管理》
